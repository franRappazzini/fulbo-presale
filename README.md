# Fulbo Pre-Sale

> **Own your club. Control your destiny.**

---

## What is Fulbo?

Fulbo is a football management and simulation game where matches are played **11 vs 11, fully controlled by AI**.

Players don't control footballers directly. Instead, they build teams, define tactics, and let the simulation decide the outcome — combining Football Manager-style strategy with AI-driven match simulation, a player-driven economy, and Web3 ownership where it genuinely adds value.

The goal is simple: create a game where strategy matters more than reflexes, every match is different, and players truly own their assets within an economy built to last for years.

---

## What is the Pre-Sale?

The Fulbo pre-sale is the first step of the project's launch strategy. It is a **stage-based token sale** where participants purchase **$FULBO tokens using SOL**, before the game goes live.

Tokens are allocated instantly but remain **locked until the Token Generation Event (TGE)**, subject to a vesting schedule that varies by stage.

The pre-sale is designed to be transparent, controlled, and predictable — giving early supporters a clear and fair entry into the Fulbo ecosystem.

---

## $FULBO Token

The **$FULBO** token is the backbone of the Fulbo economy. It is used as:

- The primary marketplace currency
- Entry fee for competitions
- Medium of exchange across the ecosystem

Token value is driven by **actual usage within the game**, not by inflation-based reward mechanics.

## Vesting

Vesting begins at TGE. The unlock schedule depends on which stage tokens were purchased in:

- **Stages 1–4:** 50% unlocked at TGE, remaining 50% unlocked linearly at 5% per month
- **Stages 5–8:** 65% unlocked at TGE, remaining 35% unlocked linearly at 5% per month
- **Stages 9–11:** 80% unlocked at TGE, remaining 20% unlocked linearly at 5% per month

The liquidity portion is locked until TGE, at which point it is used to create the initial DEX liquidity pool.

---

## Token Generation Event (TGE)

The TGE occurs when either:

- All pre-sale stages are completed, **or**
- The team sets a launch date with a minimum **30-day notice**

At TGE:
- The pre-sale closes
- Initial liquidity is deployed
- Vesting begins
- Any unsold tokens are permanently burned

## About the Game

Fulbo is built around a living, self-sustaining football economy:

- **Players** have a limited lifespan (150–250 matches), creating constant market demand
- **Scouts** generate new players and have a limited supply
- **Competitions** include a Weekly League and a Weekend Cup
- **Cosmetics** (logos, kits, stadiums) are purely visual with no gameplay impact

These mechanics — scarcity, renewal, and strategy — are designed to keep the economy active without relying on constant new users or artificial inflation.
